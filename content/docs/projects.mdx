---
title: Projects & sharing
description: Personal vs shared Projects, member roles, and moving an app between Projects.
---

A **Project** is the space your apps and their case data live in. Every account
starts with one **personal Project** — a private space that's just you — and you
can create **shared Projects** to collaborate with teammates.

## Personal Projects are private

Your personal Project is yours alone. The apps and case data in it are visible
only to you, and it **can't be shared** — there's no way to invite anyone to it.
To work with other people, you move an app into a shared Project and invite them
there.

## Shared Projects

Create a shared Project from the Project switcher at the top of the page. In a
shared Project you can invite teammates (Dimagi email addresses only), and
everyone in the Project sees the same apps and the same case data. What each
member can do is set by their **role**:

| Role | What they can do |
| --- | --- |
| **Viewer** | Open and preview the Project's apps and case data. No edits. |
| **Editor** | Build and edit the Project's apps and case data. No member management. |
| **Admin** | Everything an Editor can do, plus invite members, change their roles, and remove them. |
| **Owner** | Whoever created the Project — full control, including deleting the Project. Can't be removed. |

## Moving an app between Projects

To collaborate on an app that's in your personal Project, move it into a shared
Project. On your home page, open the **Move to Project** menu on any app and pick
the destination — the app, its case data, and its media all move together.

You can move an app between any two Projects, in either direction (including back
into your personal Project to take it private again). To move an app you must be
the **Owner** of the Project it's currently in — moving it removes it from
everyone else in that Project, so it's reserved to the owner — and at least an
**Editor** in the Project you're moving it to. (You always own your personal
Project, so apps there move freely.)
